About Us

Research group based at the Department of Materials, University of Manchester. Our offices are in the Henry Royce Institute Hub Building, where the Henry Royce Institute is the UK's Advanced Materials Research Network. The research group works on projects including silicon carbide for nuclear applications, thermal barrier coatings (TBCs) and environmental barrier coatings (EBCs).
